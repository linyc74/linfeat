<h1 align="center">LinFeat</h1>
<p align="center"><strong>Statistics with Ease</strong></p>

<p align="center">
  <a href="https://github.com/linyc74/linfeat/releases/latest">Latest release</a>
</p>

**LinFeat** is a desktop app for anyone who works with data tables—physicians, biologists, lab scientists, and other researchers—without wanting to think like a statistician all day. You bring your spreadsheet; LinFeat helps you summarize, using sensible statistical methods under the hood. The idea is to get **more insight with less effort**, while staying faithful to good practice.
